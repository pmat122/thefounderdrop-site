CRM & Automation

GoHighLevel (GHL)

Best for: Replacing 10+ tools with one platform
Editor's Pick

GoHighLevel is the Swiss Army knife for solo founders. It combines your CRM, email marketing, SMS campaigns, landing pages, appointment scheduling, and sales pipelines into one platform. Instead of paying for Calendly, Mailchimp, ClickFunnels, and a dozen other tools, you pay one flat fee and get everything.

The real power is in the automation workflows. You can build complex sequences that trigger based on behavior ‚Äî someone books a call, they automatically get a confirmation email, SMS reminder, and follow-up sequence if they no-show. The learning curve is real (give yourself a weekend), but once you've built your systems, they run themselves.

  • Full CRM with pipeline management and deal tracking
  • Built-in email, SMS, and voicemail drop campaigns
  • Landing pages and funnel builder (no coding needed)
  • Appointment scheduling with automated reminders
Pricing: Starts at $97/month (14-day free trial)

Cold Outreach

Best for: Cold email campaigns at scale

The killer feature is the warm-up tool ‚Äî it automatically warms up your email accounts by sending and receiving emails, building your sender reputation so you actually land in inboxes instead of spam.

The campaign builder makes it easy to create multi-step sequences with smart timing. You can connect unlimited email accounts and rotate sending across them, which lets you scale without burning domains. The analytics show exactly what's working ‚Äî open rates, reply rates, and which sequences convert best.

You don't need to export lists and import them elsewhere ‚Äî build your list, enrich it with verified emails, and launch sequences all in one workflow. The free tier is legitimately useful (50 emails/month), making it a great starting point.
