Automation Workflows for Pool Services

Service Day Communication

  1. 24-hour reminder: "Your pool service is scheduled for tomorrow"
  2. Morning notification with estimated arrival window
  3. En-route text 30 minutes before arrival
  4. Service completion notification with summary
  5. Any issues found → automatic repair quote request

Seasonal Opening Campaign

  1. February/March: "Spring is coming—schedule your pool opening"
  2. Include early-bird pricing incentive
  3. Follow up with non-responders at 2-week intervals
  4. Final urgency push before opening season fills up
  5. Post-opening: schedule first service and summer maintenance

Repair to Recurring Conversion

  1. Complete repair service visit
  2. Send thank you with service summary
  3. Day 3: Offer maintenance plan to prevent future issues
  4. Highlight convenience and cost savings of regular service
  5. Include review request for satisfied customers
