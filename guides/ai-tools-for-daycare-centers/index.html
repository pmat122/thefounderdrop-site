Automation Workflows for Daycare Centers

Tour Inquiry Response Sequence

  1. Inquiry received → Instant automated response within 5 minutes
  2. Include tour availability and online scheduling link
  3. If no booking in 24 hours, send follow-up with testimonials
  4. Day 3: Share virtual tour video or photo gallery
  5. Day 7: Personal outreach from director if still no booking

Post-Tour Follow-Up Sequence

  1. Same day: Thank you message with enrollment packet link
  2. Day 2: FAQ document addressing common concerns
  3. Day 4: Parent testimonial video or quotes
  4. Day 7: Check-in message asking if they have questions
  5. Day 14: Final follow-up with enrollment deadline if applicable

Waitlist Nurturing Campaign

  1. Monthly update on waitlist position and projected availability
  2. Share center news and upcoming events
  3. Invite to community events even before enrollment
  4. Alert immediately when spot becomes available
