Automation Workflows for Dojos

  1. Instant confirmation with what to expect and what to wear

  3. Post-trial follow-up within 2 hours asking about experience
  4. Day 2: Share the benefits of consistent training
  5. Day 4: Enrollment offer with urgency

  1. Track time in current belt rank
