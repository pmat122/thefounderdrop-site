Workflows That Drive Growth

The Outbound Engine

  1. Build ICP list in Apollo (filter by company size, tech stack, role)
  2. Export to Instantly
  3. Run 3-email sequence with personalized first lines (AI-generated)
  4. Positive replies auto-logged to GoHighLevel CRM
  5. Automated meeting booking through GHL calendar

The Content Flywheel

  1. Ranked identifies high-opportunity keywords
  2. Jasper generates SEO-optimized first drafts
  3. You add product-specific insights and examples
  4. Publish and track rankings in Ranked
  5. Repurpose into social content with Jasper

The Trial Conversion Sequence

  1. New trial triggers GoHighLevel workflow
  2. Day 0: Welcome email + quick start guide

  4. Day 5: Feature highlight based on their activity
  5. Day 10: Case study showing ROI
  6. Day 12: Offer to extend trial or upgrade prompt
