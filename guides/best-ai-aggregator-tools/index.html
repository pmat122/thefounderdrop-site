Why Use an AI Aggregator?

Cost savings: Individual subscriptions to ChatGPT Plus ($20), Claude Pro ($20), and Gemini Advanced ($20) total $60/month. Most aggregators cost $15-30/month for access to all three plus more.

Different AIs for different tasks: Claude excels at writing and analysis. GPT-4 is versatile and creative. Gemini has the best real-time knowledge. Switching models based on your task gets better results.

One interface to learn: Instead of juggling three different apps with different UIs, you learn one platform and access everything from there.

Side-by-side comparison: Unsure which AI gives the best answer? Query multiple models and compare. Essential for important decisions or prompt engineering.
