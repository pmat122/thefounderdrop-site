AI tools to grow your antique business with better marketing, customer outreach, and inventory descriptions.

📊 Top AI Tools for Antique Dealers

1. GoHighLevel – Collector Relationships & Alerts

Perfect for: Want lists, new inventory alerts, estate sale notifications, customer follow-ups

GoHighLevel helps you build relationships with collectors. Create want list systems that alert buyers when you get pieces they're looking for. Send new inventory notifications to interested collectors. Build campaigns for estate sale opportunities and maintain relationships with consignors.

How Antique Dealers Use GoHighLevel:

  • Want list system: "You're looking for Art Deco chairs? When I find them, I'll contact you first."
  • Automatic alerts: A collector opts in, and every time you list furniture from the 1950s, they get notified
  • Consignor relationships: Track which dealers you source from, send them leads, manage commission splits
  • Buyers follow-up: After a sale, follow-up sequences build repeat business ("New inventory in your favorite era just arrived")

2. Jasper AI – Listings & Content

Perfect for: Detailed item descriptions, blog posts about periods/styles, social media, newsletters

Jasper helps you write the detailed descriptions that sell antiques. Create compelling listings with historical context, provenance details, and condition notes. Write blog posts about furniture styles, pottery marks, and collecting tips. Generate social media content showcasing your inventory.

Real-World Antique Dealer Uses:

  • eBay listings: Instead of generic "antique chair," write "Victorian Walnut Parlor Chair, c. 1880s, with authentic caning and original finish"
  • Blog posts: "How to Identify Art Deco Furniture" or "Understanding Maker's Marks in Fine Porcelain" — rank on Google, attract collectors
  • Provenance documentation: Generate detailed historical context for high-value pieces (helps sell and improves insurance value)
  • Monthly newsletter: "What's New This Month + Collecting Tips" keeps collectors engaged between purchases

3. Magai – Multi-Model AI for Research & Writing

Perfect for: Research assistance, attribution help, detailed documentation

Magai helps with research and documentation tasks. Research makers' marks, historical periods, and provenance. Create detailed documentation for insurance purposes. Perfect for the deep research that quality antique dealing requires. Use it to verify maker attributions, research historical context, and document conditions for high-value pieces.

Documentation Applications:

  • Maker research: "Is this mark from Meissen or Sèvres?" — Magai helps verify attribution
  • Condition reports: Generate detailed condition documentation for insurance and buyer confidence
  • Provenance tracking: Document the history and ownership trail of valuable pieces

4. Apollo.io – Find Interior Designers & Dealers

Perfect for: Interior designers, estate attorneys, other dealers for trading

Apollo helps you find trade buyers and referral sources. Target interior designers who buy for clients, connect with estate attorneys who handle liquidations, and find other dealers for trading inventory. Build a network of professional contacts who send you business consistently.

Networking Strategy:

  • Interior designers: They buy from you regularly for client projects — find and contact them directly
  • Estate attorneys: When clients pass and estates liquidate, attorneys connect with dealers — build those relationships
  • Specialty dealers: Find dealers in complementary categories (if you do Victorian, they do Art Deco) for trading

  • Want trade buyers? → Apollo + Instantly for outreach
  • Need more collectors finding you? → Ranked for SEO
