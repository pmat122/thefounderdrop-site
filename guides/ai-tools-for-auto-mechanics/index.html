Automation Workflows for Repair Shops

Service Reminder Sequence

  1. Track last service date and mileage for each customer
  2. Send reminder text 2 weeks before estimated next service due
  3. Include online scheduling link with current availability
  4. If no response in 5 days, follow up with value-add (free inspection)
  5. After 30 days overdue, escalate to phone call from service advisor

Repair Approval Workflow

  1. Technician finds additional needed repairs
  2. Service advisor sends text with photos/video and estimate
  3. Customer approves via text link or calls back
  4. Automated reminder if no response in 2 hours
  5. End-of-day follow-up if still pending

Post-Service Review Collection

  1. Send thank you text 2 hours after pickup
  2. Ask about satisfaction with quick rating
  3. Happy customers get Google review request link
  4. Less than happy customers get manager follow-up

Revenue-Boosting AI Strategies

Declined Service Recovery

Track repairs customers decline and follow up strategically:

  • Send safety reminders for declined brake/suspension work
  • Offer financing options for expensive repairs
  • Time follow-ups before seasonal needs (AC in spring, heat in fall)
  • Include educational content explaining why the service matters

State Inspection Campaigns

Inspections bring customers in the door for upsells:

  • Track inspection expiration dates for all customers
  • Send reminders 30, 14, and 7 days before expiration
  • Include appointment booking link
  • Follow up with service recommendations after inspection
