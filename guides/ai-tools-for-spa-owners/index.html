Automation Workflows for Spas

Pre-Appointment Experience

  1. Booking confirmation with treatment details and what to expect
  2. 48-hour reminder with arrival instructions
  3. Day-of reminder with parking and check-in information
  4. For first-time clients: intake form and spa orientation

Post-Treatment Follow-Up

  1. Thank you message 2 hours after appointment
  2. Treatment-specific aftercare instructions
  3. Day 3: Check on results and experience

Membership Engagement

  4. 60-day renewal reminder sequence
  5. Upgrade campaigns for active members
