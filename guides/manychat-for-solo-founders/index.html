Step 1: The Caption Hook

Every post includes a call-to-action with a trigger word:

  • "Comment GUIDE for the full breakdown"

Step 2: The Automation Flow

When someone comments the trigger word, ManyChat instantly sends a DM:

"Hey! Here's the toolkit I mentioned 👇

Before I send it—what's your email? I'll send the full version there too (it's got more detail than I can fit in a DM)."

Step 3: Email Capture

They reply with their email. ManyChat captures it, adds them to my email list via Zapier, and sends them the resource. All automatic.
