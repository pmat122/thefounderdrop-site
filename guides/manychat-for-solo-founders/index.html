How I Set Up My Comment Trigger Automation

Let me walk you through my exact setup. This is the same system that brought in those 47 subscribers while I was sleeping.

Step 1: Create Your Trigger Keywords

I use specific, memorable keywords that match what I'm offering in the post. For example:

  • TOOLKIT — for my founder tools checklist
  • GUIDE — for my detailed how-to guides
  • TEMPLATE — for swipe files and templates
  • CHECKLIST — for quick-reference checklists

The key is making the word simple and related to what you're offering. Don't make people type a complicated phrase. One word. All caps. Easy to remember.

Step 2: Build Your Automation Flow

In ManyChat, I create a flow that triggers when someone comments the keyword on a specific post. The flow looks like this:

  1. Trigger: Comment contains "TOOLKIT" on [specific post]
  2. Message 1: "Hey! 👋 Thanks for grabbing the toolkit. Quick question before I send it over—what's the best email to send this to?"
  3. Email capture: User types their email
  4. Message 2: "Perfect! Check your inbox in the next few minutes. While you wait—what's your biggest challenge right now with [topic]?"
  5. Deliver link: Send the actual lead magnet URL

Notice how the flow does two things: captures the email AND starts a conversation. That second question often leads to real engagement that turns cold leads into warm prospects.

Step 3: Connect to Your Email List

ManyChat integrates with most email platforms. I have mine connected so that every captured email automatically gets tagged and added to a specific nurture sequence. No manual exports. No copy-pasting. It just works.
