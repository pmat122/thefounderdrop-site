Automation Workflows for Optometry

Annual Exam Recall Sequence

  1. GoHighLevel identifies patients due for annual exam (11 months from last visit)
  2. Automated text: "Hi [Name], it's time for your annual eye exam! Your vision benefits reset soon."
  3. Email follows with online booking link
  4. If no response in 7 days, second reminder with insurance urgency
  5. Non-responders get a personal call from staff

Contact Lens Reorder Automation

  1. Track when patient's contact lens supply should run low (based on last order)
  2. Send reorder reminder 2 weeks before projected run-out date
  3. Include easy reorder link or auto-ship enrollment option
  4. Follow up with prescription expiration warning if needed

Post-Visit Review Collection

  1. Send satisfaction survey 24 hours after appointment
  2. Happy patients get direct link to leave Google review
  3. Neutral/negative feedback routes to practice manager for resolution
