Automation Workflows for Pharmacies

Medication Adherence Sequence

  1. Track patient fill patterns for maintenance medications
  2. Send friendly reminder 5 days before prescription runs out
  3. If no refill in 3 days, escalate with call option
  4. Non-adherent patients get pharmacist outreach call
  5. Document interventions for MTM billing

Immunization Campaign Workflow

  1. Segment patients by age and immunization eligibility
  2. Send targeted campaigns (flu for all, shingles for 50+, pneumonia for 65+)
  3. Include scheduling link and insurance information
  4. Follow up with reminder for those who didn't respond
  5. Track vaccination rates for quality reporting

New Patient Onboarding

  1. Welcome message with pharmacy services overview
  2. Offer to transfer remaining prescriptions
  3. Introduce specialty services (delivery, compounding, MTM)
  4. Request Google review after first positive interaction
