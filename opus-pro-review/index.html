What Is Opus Pro?

Opus Pro (formerly Opus Clip) is an AI-powered video clipping tool that transforms long-form content into viral short-form clips. Upload a YouTube video, podcast episode, or webinar ‚Äî and Opus Pro automatically identifies the most engaging moments and creates ready-to-post clips for TikTok, YouTube Shorts, Instagram Reels, and LinkedIn.

The magic is in the AI's ability to detect "hook-worthy" moments, add dynamic captions, and even predict which clips have the highest viral potential with its proprietary "Virality Score." For content creators drowning in editing work, Opus Pro is a massive time-saver.

If you're creating long-form content and want to 10x your output without 10x the editing hours, Opus Pro is built for you.

Final Verdict

Opus Pro is a game-changer for content creators who produce long-form videos but struggle to keep up with the short-form content demands of TikTok, Reels, and Shorts. The AI genuinely saves hours of work ‚Äî what used to take half a day of scrubbing and editing now happens in minutes.

The Virality Score feature is more than a gimmick; it actually helps you prioritize which clips to post. Combined with auto-captions and social scheduling, Opus Pro creates a complete content repurposing workflow.

Who should use it: YouTubers, podcasters, course creators, and anyone repurposing long-form content into short-form clips.

Who should skip: Creators who only make short-form content, or those with very niche/technical content that AI might misinterpret.
