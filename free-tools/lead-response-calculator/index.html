How to Fix Your Response Time

1. Set up instant auto-replies

When a lead submits a form, send an automated text and email within 60 seconds acknowledging their inquiry. This alone can increase conversion by 30-50%.

2. Use a CRM with built-in automation

Tools like GoHighLevel can trigger automated follow-up sequences the moment a lead comes in — texts, emails, and even voicemail drops.

3. Route leads to the right person instantly

Use round-robin assignment or territory-based routing so leads never sit in a shared inbox waiting. Every minute counts.

4. Follow up 5+ times in the first 48 hours

The data is clear: most deals close after the 5th follow-up. Use automated email sequences so no lead falls through the cracks.
