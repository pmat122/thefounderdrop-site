Final Verdict

Instantly has earned its spot as the #1 cold email tool for solo founders. The unlimited email accounts model is a game-changer‚Äîyou can scale outreach without costs spiraling. The warmup and deliverability features are genuinely best-in-class.

The main limitation is that you need to bring your own leads. Instantly doesn't have a contact database like Apollo. The ideal setup for many founders is Apollo for finding leads + Instantly for sending campaigns.

Who should buy: Founders doing cold email outreach who want unlimited sending capacity and maximum deliverability.

Who should skip: Those who need a lead database included, or who only send occasional outreach emails.
