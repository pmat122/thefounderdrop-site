What Is ElevenLabs?

ElevenLabs is the AI voice platform that's redefined what's possible with synthetic speech. Their text-to-speech technology produces voices so realistic that they're often indistinguishable from human recordings.

But the killer feature is voice cloning. Upload a few minutes of audio, and ElevenLabs creates a digital clone of that voice. Content creators are using this to narrate videos in their own voice without recording, translate content into other languages while keeping their voice, and scale audio production infinitely.

For solo founders creating content, ElevenLabs opens up possibilities that were previously impossible or prohibitively expensive: audiobook narration, video voiceovers, podcast intros, and multilingual content‚Äîall without a recording studio.
