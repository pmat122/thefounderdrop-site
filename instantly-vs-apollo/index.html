Instantly Overview

Instantly has become the go-to cold email tool for solo founders and agencies alike. Its killer feature? Unlimited email accounts at every price tier. This lets you scale your outreach without worrying about sending limits.

The built-in email warmup is genuinely excellent‚Äîit automatically warms new email accounts and maintains sender reputation. The deliverability dashboard shows you exactly where your emails are landing (inbox vs. spam vs. promotions).

What Instantly lacks is a lead database. You need to bring your own leads or integrate with tools like Apollo. But for pure cold email execution, nothing beats it.

  • Unlimited email accounts on all plans
  • Industry-leading warmup and deliverability
  • Smart sequences with send-time optimization
  • A/B testing and analytics built-in
  • AI-powered email writer for campaigns

Apollo Overview

Apollo is the lead generation powerhouse. With access to 275M+ contacts and 60M+ companies, it's essentially a B2B database, enrichment tool, and outreach platform rolled into one.

The prospecting features are unmatched. You can filter leads by industry, company size, job title, technologies used, funding stage, and dozens of other criteria. The email enrichment accuracy is among the best in the industry.

Apollo also includes sequence automation, but its deliverability tools aren't as robust as Instantly's. Many power users find leads in Apollo, then export and run campaigns in Instantly.

  • 275M+ contact database
  • Advanced filtering and search
  • Email and phone enrichment
  • Built-in CRM functionality
  • Chrome extension for LinkedIn prospecting

Who Should Use Instantly

  • Founders focused on cold email at scale
  • Anyone who already has a lead list
  • Those prioritizing deliverability above all
  • Agencies managing multiple client accounts
  • Teams running high-volume outreach campaigns

Who Should Use Apollo

  • Founders who need to find leads from scratch
  • B2B businesses targeting specific industries
  • Sales teams needing CRM + outreach in one
  • Anyone who needs phone numbers for cold calling
  • LinkedIn prospectors (Chrome extension is great)

Our Pick: Use Both Together

The power move for 2026: Use Apollo's free tier to find and enrich leads, then export to Instantly for email campaigns. You get the best lead database + the best deliverability. Start with Instantly if you have leads, Apollo if you need them.
