Opus Pro Overview

Opus Pro is the AI-powered clip creator that's taken the content world by storm. Upload a long-form video, and it uses AI to automatically find the most engaging moments and turn them into short-form clips ready for TikTok, Reels, and Shorts.

The magic is in the "Virality Score"‚ÄîOpus Pro analyzes your content and predicts which clips are most likely to perform well. It adds dynamic captions, reframes video for vertical formats, and handles everything automatically.

For solo founders creating YouTube content or podcasts, Opus Pro is a game-changer. What used to take hours of manual editing now happens in minutes.

  • AI finds the best moments automatically
  • Virality Score predicts top performers
  • Dynamic captions and emoji integration
  • Multiple template styles for branding
  • Batch processing for multiple clips

Repurpose IO Overview

Repurpose IO is the content distribution machine. It doesn't create clips for you‚Äîinstead, it automates the process of publishing your content across 20+ platforms simultaneously.

Connect your YouTube channel, podcast RSS, or content source, and Repurpose IO automatically distributes new content to TikTok, Instagram, LinkedIn, Twitter, Facebook, and more. You can set up custom workflows that trigger based on new uploads.

Where Repurpose IO shines is automation. Set it once, and your content flows to every platform without manual uploading. It's particularly strong for podcasters who want to distribute audio content everywhere.

  • 20+ platform integrations
  • Automated workflow triggers
  • Works with video AND audio content
  • Custom scheduling per platform
  • No daily upload limits on higher plans
